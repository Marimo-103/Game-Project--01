Let's Play a Game!

Before we begin, we must know the rules of this game.

  • It is a Double Player game
  • Players must ENTER their name before starting the game
  • Player One will have an opportunity to enter only one number between 01-10
  • Player Two will have 5 opportunities to guess what number PLAYER One has entered
  • If, PLAYER Two guessed the correct number that was entered by PLAYER One then Player Two Wins, else PLAYER One Wins
